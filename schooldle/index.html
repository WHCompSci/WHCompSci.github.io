How to play

Try to guess the daily college in as few tries as possible. Tiles change color to show proximity to the answer.

Color System:

  • Green: Correct match in any column.
  • Yellow (Acceptance Rate): Correct rate within 5% of guess.
  • Yellow (Region, State & Public/Private): No yellow color.
  • Yellow (Total Enrollment and Total Cost): Correct numbers within 5 % (students, dollars) of guess.

Normal Mode:

Guess a well-known college in the United States in 8 tries or less.

State Mode:

Guess any college in a state in 6 tries or less.
